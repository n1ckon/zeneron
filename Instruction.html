Instructions for Use

  1. Install a new battery in the earpiece with the "+" sign in the direction of the security thread used to remove the microearpiece from your ear canal.

    2. Use only alkaline watch batteries type AG0 (SR63, SR521W, L63, 379, LR521). Sony, Maxwell, Camelion, Golden Power, and Hyundai are the watch battery brands best suited for the microearpiece. Other, even more expensive batteries may contain silver oxide which causes strong static and hissing.

  2. Clean your ear canal thoroughly using a cotton swab. Insert the earpiece into your ear canal. A slight hissing sound will let you know that the microearpiece is activated. Always clean your ear canal before using the microearpiece. This simple procedure will prevent ninety percent of all non-warranty microearpiece breakdowns.
  3. Connect the Bluetooth headset to your mobile phone.
    • Activate the Bluetooth headset.
    • On your mobile phone, go to Settings and select Bluetooth.
    • Enable Bluetooth and press Search for New Device. Select the found device and connect. If promoted enter the code "0000”.
  4. Place the induction coil antenna with Bluetooth headset around your neck. Set the sound volume to a comfortable level (adjustable both on the Bluetooth headset and on your phone). Set your phone in silent mode and dial your assistant’s phone number. Your spy microearpiece is now ready to use.
