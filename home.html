ZENERON Labs is a surveillance and communications equipment manufacturer. We design and build professional grade communication and systems for security services companies and law enforcement. Our expertise stems from our consulting work in building communication systems for our enterprise clients. All ZENERON brand equipment is based on the technical solutions synthesized from our extensive experience. All equipment is thoroughly tested and tuned for thousands of hours both in-house and on the field. ZENERON Labs prides itself in offering the best technical solutions for its clients’ communication and surveillance needs. ZENERON Labs’ longstanding partnerships with clients are testimony to our commitment to the highest standards of quality and reliability.
