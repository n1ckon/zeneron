Introduction

The Zeneron Bluetooth Spy Microearpiece is a hidden high-fidelity communication system. It is ideal for undetectable two-way communication using your mobile phone. No additional equipment needed. It allows you to communicate covertly with a helper in situations where open communication is impossible. This is a reliable professional grade system developed for law enforcement and security services use.

Common usage cases include: security and bodyguard activities, exam and test-taking, public speaking, business negotiations, and law enforcement.

The Zeneron Bluetooth Microearpiece set is a custom system designed from the best components available on the market. It is derivative of the systems developed by Zeneron in consulting for security services companies. The system has been tested for thousands of hours to ensure superior quality, reliability, and ease of use.

Technical Specifications

Radius

The Zeneron Bluetooth Microeapiece communication system is based on Bluetooth technology. The system requires the use of a mobile phone compatible with the Bluetooth headset. It will operate wherever there is cellphone signal. While using the system the mobile phone may be up to 30 feet away from the wearer. The micro earpiece may be up to four feet away from the induction coil antenna attached to the Bluetooth headset, but only in the absence of any electromagnetic interference.

Duration of Use

The microearpiece may operate anywhere from four to twelve hours using a fresh alkaline battery. The duration of use depends on the quality of the battery. A fully charged Bluetooth headset will be operational for ten to twelve hours if in active use.

Sound quality

The microearpiece is configured to deliver the best possible sound quality with minimal interference. It is anatomically shaped and flesh-colored in order to be easily concealed. It is only loud enough for the wearer to hear, but undetectable to a bystander.
